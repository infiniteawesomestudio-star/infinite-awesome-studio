What We Collect

We collect only what you voluntarily give us:

Cookies

This site does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. Cloudflare may set a security cookie (__cf_bm) for bot protection. No personal data is stored in that cookie.

Data Retention

Contact and booking inquiries are retained only as long as needed to fulfill the purpose of your request. If you would like your information removed, email us and we will delete it promptly.
