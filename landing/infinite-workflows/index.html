What Your Front Desk Handles

Everything between "hey, you open?" and a paid, booked appointment.

It lives on the channels your clients already use. Instagram, Facebook, text, your booking page. When a message comes in, it goes to work.

01

Answers instantly, in your voice

Every message and comment gets a reply in seconds, day or night, sounding like you and your business. No more messages sitting for hours.

02

Handles the repeat questions

Pricing, availability, what to expect, deposits, your policies. The stuff you answer twenty times a day, answered for you.

03

Books the appointment

It reads your calendar, offers real open slots, and locks in the booking. The client picks a time without a back-and-forth.

04

Collects the deposit

The slot isn't held until the deposit's in. Fewer no-shows, less chasing and the money lands in your account, not mine.

05

Sends the reminders

Automatic nudges before the appointment so people actually show up, without you setting a single alarm.

06

Answers the phone too, if you want

Most of your booking lives in messages, so that's where we start. Want calls picked up as well? We can add that on.
