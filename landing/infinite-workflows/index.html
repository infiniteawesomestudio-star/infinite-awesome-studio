How the Launchpad Works

Five steps. One focused engagement. Three working workflows.

1

Workflow Audit (60 minutes)

We map where your time actually goes. I ask about your biggest admin bottlenecks, your current tools and what you wish an assistant could handle. You leave with a clear picture of your top three workflows ranked by impact and ease.

2

AI Toolkit Setup

I walk you through everything you need to get your AI tools configured correctly: what to use, what to connect and what not to put in without a process. No guessing, no YouTube rabbit holes.

3

Build Three Workflows

We build your top three workflows together, built around your real business, your actual documents and your language. Each one gets a reusable prompt and a one-page SOP.

4

Train You and Your Team

A focused 60-minute training session where you and your team run each workflow, ask questions and practice until it's second nature. The goal is confidence, not dependency.

5

Seven-Day Follow-Up

A week after the launchpad, I check back in. We troubleshoot, adjust and make sure the workflows are actually being used. If something isn't working, we fix it.

"You're not getting a demo. You're getting three workflows that work in your business, documented so you can run them without me in the room."
